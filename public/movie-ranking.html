The Best Movies According to Joseph

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ace Ventura: Pet Detective

This is one of the funniest movies. Over the top comedy that is simple and easy to laugh with your friends.

The Matrix

Great sci-fi action movie that makes you think twice about the world you live in.

Lord of the Rings

This epic triliogy of high adventure allows you to explore the imaginative world of fantasy long after you watch the movie.
